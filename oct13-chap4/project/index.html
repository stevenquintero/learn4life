Frank Ocean

Steven Quintero | October 13, 2016 10:06 am

Visionary artist Frank Ocean has earned his place as one of the most celebrated and critically acclaimed acts of this era; a timeless icon. ‘Endless’, his third album to date, dropped as one of the most anticipated projects of all time. The record follows his heavily lauded breakout records; the grammy-award winning ‘Channel Orange’ and the ‘Nostalgia Ultra’ mixtape.
